How Crypto Payments Work in Bhutan
Three simple steps — no bank needed, no cash required
1
🏪
Merchant Shows QR Code
The shop owner displays their unique wallet QR code with the price in Ngultrum. No special equipment needed — just a phone.
2
📱
Customer Scans and Pays
The customer opens their crypto wallet app, scans the QR code, and confirms the payment. Takes less than 30 seconds.
3
✅
Blockchain Confirms
The Bitcoin network confirms the transaction. Both parties receive instant confirmation. No middleman, no bank fees.
